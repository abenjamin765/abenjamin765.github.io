Media Manager

A better way to bring all of your digital media together

My Role

I led the design of Media Manager for iOS and Android from the begining of the project in November 2015.

I was responsible for planning and excuting the design of a new product based on market research and an evaluation of customer needs.

Project Summary

Our target user needs to be able to curate their content into a single destination and automate workflows to distribute content quickly over multiple channels according to business logic.

Research showed that the average digital marketer stores and manages media on three or more channels and uses multiple technologies to distribute it.

InCar UI Design Concept

Stay focused on what matters.

Project Summary

Computers in cars? Why not. This idea along with wearable tech and other "on-the-go" technology is ushering in a new paradigm of the computer interface. Why touch when you can talk? The inCar display shows only key information and alerts like the time, in-car temp, and sound volume.

The visual cue "Hey Car" at the bottom left signals the verbal cue that activates the main menu. After it is learned, there's no longer a need for the user to look at the screen again unless they're using a new app or menu. Eyes on the road folks!

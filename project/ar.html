Augmented Reality App Redesign

Scan images to bring them to life!

My Role

I provided interaction and visual design support for the redesign of an existing Augmented Reality product for iOS.

My role was to help users connect to the application through on-screen visuals and interactions.

Project Summary

User feedback from the App Store and input from stakeholders pointed to a visual refresh of the existing product.

The app is used to scan and reveal user-generated AR content created in-app or in the advanced web editor.

My goal was to bring the visuals inline with modern design patterns and styles that would be familiar to our users.

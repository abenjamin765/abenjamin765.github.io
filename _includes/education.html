Education

College

In 2008, I went to an awesome school in Orlando, Florida called Full Sail University to learn about what makes a designer. I learned that there was a ton more to being a designer than being clever with Photoshop. Another thing that stuck with me since my college experience is that learning doesn't stop when you leave the classroom.

Online Courses

I began my career making graphics for everything from websites to billboards. As I started picking up landing pages, apps, and more complex products, I started to learn new skills in order to focus more on the bigger picture.

Using online courses, I dove into coding, business, analytics, testing, and started to teach myself everything I could about the rest of the software development process.

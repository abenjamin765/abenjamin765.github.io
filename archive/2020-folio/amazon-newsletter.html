Amazon Seller NewsletterA better way to help Amazon Sellers stay up-to-date and serve customers

TL;DR

Selling products online is a complex process with numerous moving parts to keep track of. What if Amazon compiled useful news, insights, and tips and shared that information with Sellers?

Introduction

Feedback & Insights

  • Communication from Amazon is often perceived as negative
  • News and updates are delivered to Sellers across multiple channels
  • Sellers feel there's a lack of official insight from Amazon
  • Policy and security updates are often discovered when it's too late to act
  • General communication with Amazon feels 1-way
  • Sellers across markets feel disconnected from each-other
"Every email from Amazon feels like a punch right in the gut." —Amazon Seller

My role

  • Gather insights from Amazon Sellers
  • Craft a meaningful narrative
  • Design a visual layout
  • Align customer needs with business objectives
  • Execute the production of the newsletter
  • Send the newsletter

Strategy

Goals & Objectives

  • Centralize disparate communications
  • Improve Seller Trust Index Score
  • Maintain a 3.5/5 or better "How's My Driving?" Score
  • Improve response to security updates
  • Increase awareness of policy changes
  • Encourage customer-centric behavior

Components

Story: Highlights from detailed stories relevant to sellers. Each card will link to a full article in Seller Central.

Seller-to-Seller Tip: Advice sellers want to broadcast to other sellers.

Policy update: Important policy updates that impact sellers.

How's my driving?: Link to the "How's my driving?"" survey.

Execution

Pilot Episode

Feedback

"So glad this is in my inbox. I think I'll keep it." —Amazon Seller
  • Buttons don't always behave as expected in some email clients
  • Images should be more consistent
  • Seller's want a browser view
  • Subscription controls are needed in Seller Central
  • It's not clear how often this will be sent out
  • Seller's need to be able to view articles without logging in
  • Feedback responses were lower than expected

Episode 2: North America

  • Replaced buttons with text links
  • Introduced policy card
  • Favored photo-real images
  • Made linked content available publicly
  • Added a more explicit call-out for feedback
  • Added newsletter subscription to Seller Central profile

Scaling up & going global

The next phase involved scaling the newsletter up to 12 markets and translating content and imagery to 6 different languages.

  • Source global and localized content
  • Identify market stakeholders
  • Engage translation team
  • Update design to consider right-to-left languages
  • Localize branding
  • Expand research efforts

Outcomes

  • The Seller Newsletter was successfully launched worldwide across 12 markets and translated to 6 languages in May 2017 and continues to be released monthly to-date
  • Open-rate exceeded expectations at 34% with the first edition and a 0.5% increase month-to-month
  • "How's my driving?" score averaged 4.2/5 in the first 6 months
  • Seller Trust Index showed positive impact after 3 months with continued influence
  • Feedback from the newsletter survey provides valuable insights that help shape new features of the newsletter and Marketplace itself
  • The newsletter efforts helped to inform and shape the broader communication strategy with sellers
  • After the 4th newsletter, internal distribution started to the Amazon Marketplace organization and to the executive leadership team
  • The Seller Newsletter is being used to communicate important information to sellers regarding the 2020 pandemic response

Adobe Jam Atlanta - Trolley

The awesome folks at Adobe came to Atlanta and invited designers to go head-to-head in a design challenge: Design an app from concept to prototype in just 3 hours that helps the Atlanta traffic problem. I teamed up with Bryan Lewis to create Trolly; A work-place on wheels that commuters connect to from a mobile app to signal that they need a lift to work. Once on-board, Wi-fi is available to allow riders to have a productive or enjoyable journey to the office.
